Jared Aguayo

Computer Science and Engineering

Engineering leads to innovation and advancement of technology in all fields. It uses previously known knowledge to build on existing ideas and to create entirely new ones. Engineering lets a person create. A person can do things never done before, and working in teams is significant in engineering. Contributing to a team to make something one could not accomplish on their own is rewarding. Engineering results in the continual progression and betterment of humanity. My passion is engineering.

I personally enjoy the concept of computer science and engineering because it is so versatile. With the evolving of technology, almost all useful devices now contain some connection to software. This means that many companies and corporations are searching for people with these expertise. Not only this, but all you need for the computer science part is a some kind of computing device such as a laptop. You can create new programs from virtually anywhere and anytime. In this digital age, I want to strive to make a difference.

Electrical Engineering

I chose engineering because I knew I would never be satisfied doing anything else. There are so many options that a degree in engineering makes possible: I could find a job nearly anywhere in the world because of how many applications there are for the skills engineers have, I could work for huge companies or do research in a university setting, or even donate my skills to a cause I believe in. A B.S. in engineering is one of the most versatile degrees anyone can get, and I need a career that would gratify my intellectual side as well as my adventurous side. I don't want to settle into a single job for the rest of my life if I can avoid it.

I want to be an electrical engineer because I love science and technology and I am a born problem solver.

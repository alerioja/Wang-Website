The Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE) improves humanity through technological innovation. They make it a global community in order to share and further technology as well as better world conditions. IEEE, as the name provides, has a large focus in the fields of electronics.

National Society of Professional Engineers

Started in 1934, NSPE stands as the voice for licensed Professional Engineers and Engineering Interns. Their main goal is to allow their members to practice engineering ethically and professionally. To accomplish this task, NSPE provides education, leadership training, and networking for all their members.

Computing Research Association

CRA wants to improve technology by gaining support with industry, government, and academia to produce more research and education in computing. They do this by informing policy makers and the public, leading the computing world in research, and creating more talent in the fields of computer science and computer engineering.

IEEE Computer Society

Because IEEE deals with electronics, a huge part of the organization revolves around computers. IEEE Computer Society gives information, training, and community. Information includes publications, vast digital libraries, and technical conferences. They have training to develop leaders for the technical world or even to enhance the performance of current company employees. The community is very diverse ranging from students and professors to software engineers and researchers.

Google

Google focuses on the user and wants technology to cater to the userâ€™s needs. Google is primarily focused on software heavy projects, which means they tend to hire computer science, computer engineering, and electrical engineering majors.

Google looks for creative talent. Innovation comes from people thinking outside the box and Google likes to see this in its employees. Another highly sought after trait is being able to work in teams. Almost every project is a team effort at Google. A typical work day at Google would include getting to work, going to a meeting, and programming at your own pace as long as you meet the deadline.

Raytheon

Raytheon specializes in defense and security. They have many products that revolve around sensing, communications, and mission support. Raytheon supports many types of jobs and employs 65,000 worldwide. With this number, it is no surprise that electrical and computer engineers are needed at Raytheon.

Programming as well as the knowledge to physically put the technology together is essential for the job. When dealing with defense and security, it is imperative that there are no preventable mistakes. Engineering requires you to work as a group, and Raytheon is no exception.

Broadcom

Broadcom helps bring video and other multimedia to the customer, no matter if one is at home, the office, or even a mobile environment. They use both wired and wireless connections. To connect with the customer, programming and other electronics are needed, around 77% of the company are engineers. Working at Broadcom would be much like the other companies. Go to meetings, program, work as a group, and maybe help develop new chips or devices.

  • About Broadcom

Microsoft

Microsoft is involved with almost all aspects of computers. Many people know them for their software products such as Microsoft Office and Windows. However, they also work on things like keyboards, mice, the Xbox, and the Windows phone. Like Google, Microsoft is looking for innovative people who can perform at top levels. Working in groups is a must, and they tend to target computer science, computer engineering, and electrical engineering majors. Working at Microsoft would vary depending on your project, but almost all projects require the use of programming.

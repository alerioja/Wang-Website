Software Engineer

Currently one of the most in-demand, highly rewarding career. This trend is not stopping anytime soon.

A software engineer creates applications, systems and programs that are not only user-friendly but also productivity-boosters. The wide array of devices that can be implemented range from smartphones, computers, cars, planes, etc.

Power Systems Engineers

Power Systems Engineers control the generation, transmission, and distribution of electrical energy. With a large portion of current employees approaching retirement age, there will be a huge demand for this type of engineer in the future. Because of emerging “green” technologies such as solar and wind energy there will be an endless supply of opportunities for power engineers over the next few years. As the demand for energy continues to increase, power engineers will be needed to solve the problems of America’s energy crisis.

  • I want to put all my energy into this!

Computer Hardware Engineers

They research, design, and test computer equipment, always trying to make the next generation of devices faster, more powerful, and more efficient. Computer Hardware Engineers work at the cutting edge of engineering often times guiding the industry through rapid changes in computer technology. If you are interested in how computers work at the most fundamental level computer hardware engineering may be right for you.

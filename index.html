About engineering


This is what it's about;
#math #thinking

Engineering is the application of Mathematics and Science to solve problems. Engineers design, build and test all kinds of products. They play a major role in developing technology that enhances our lives from the smartphones in our pockets to the satellites in outer space. Our modern life is made possible by advances in engineering.

Make. An. Impact.

Engineering is an innovative process that has brought about radical changes in communication, entertainment, travel, medicine, and infrastructure. Although the work can be very demanding, people willing to pursue a career in engineering will find that it is a very rewarding field. Engineers are well compensated and their work has an immediate and real impact on millions of people around the world.
